Bjorn Ethan Marai A Papua New Guinean Creative. I Capture Life

I am Bjorn Marai, a proud photographer and videographer hailing from the stunning landscapes of Papua New Guinea. Through my lens, I strive to capture the essence of our remarkable natural surroundings and the vibrant tapestry of our diverse cultures. It is my fervent belief that through the power of visual storytelling, I can share the beauty and uniqueness of my homeland with the world, fostering a deeper appreciation for the rich heritage and breathtaking landscapes that define Papua New Guinea. Thank you for joining me on this journey of discovery through the art of photography and videography.
